❓ Help & Instructions
How to manage your Lucky Fresh Mart website.
📱 How WhatsApp Orders Work

When a customer clicks "Order on WhatsApp", they are taken to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing their cart items. You simply reply to confirm the order and arrange delivery.

💡 Make sure to set your correct WhatsApp number in Settings!
💰 How to Update Prices
  1. Go to Products page
  2. Find the vegetable you want to update
  3. Type the new price directly in the price field
  4. Click "Save All Price Changes" button
  5. Prices update instantly on the website!
🔥 Setting Daily Offers
  1. Go to Offers page
  2. Delete yesterday's offers with the ❌ button
  3. Add new offers with today's special deals
  4. Offers appear instantly on your website homepage
